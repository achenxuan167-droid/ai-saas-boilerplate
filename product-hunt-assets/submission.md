## Description（产品介绍）

I got tired of wiring up Stripe webhooks and Supabase auth for every new AI project. So I built this.

AISaaS is a production-ready Next.js boilerplate that bundles everything you need to launch an AI-powered SaaS:

🔐 **Supabase Auth** — Magic link + Google OAuth out of the box. No more auth boilerplate.

💰 **Usage-Based Stripe Billing** — Per-token, per-call, or per-seat pricing. Metered billing, webhook handling, and subscription management all wired up.

📊 **Dashboard** — Live usage charts, subscription status, billing history. Looks good on day one.

🎨 **Landing Page + Pricing** — SEO-optimized landing page and pricing table. Launch without a separate marketing site.

🧠 **AI-Ready Architecture** — Streaming response patterns, token tracking middleware. Ready to plug into Claude, OpenAI, or any API.

🚀 **One-Click Vercel Deploy** — Clone, set env vars, deploy. From zero to live in under 30 minutes.

**Tech Stack**: Next.js 16, Tailwind v4, Supabase, Stripe, TypeScript

**$99 one-time. Lifetime updates. No subscription.**
